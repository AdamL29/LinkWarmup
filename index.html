Here are 3 great places to search for information.

Google

Google is a great source for searching anything on the web. It gives multiple pages of sites regarding the information you are searching for.

Wikipedia

Wikipedia is a knowledge source, however, it can have false information as it is open to the public to edit the pages of any material searched for. Make sure to follow up on the resources on the bottom of the pages to ensure they are correct.

Youtube

Youtube is a great source for How To videos. As a website made to share content, it gives many people help when visual help is needed for the viewer.
